The B BIJOUX brand brings together three ranges of jewelery, all created and manufactured in our workshops near La Rochelle. For women, the Baïn range offers creations based on colored resins, ceramics and evolves its collections every year. The men's jewelery, under the Bô bijoux banner, is available in a wide range of colors and cords with an urban chic, boho or marine spirit. The latest addition to B BIJOUX, the Lorosso bracelets are available in three metal colors. Leather or rope, plain or two-tone, they boldly display a contemporary style, graphic and design.
